𝐒𝐈𝐊𝐋𝐎 (Cycle) | A Short Film

Uno Films

The short film entitled "𝐒𝐈𝐊𝐋𝐎" centers on an activist named Ajax, who was sent as a boy named Agapito during the Spanish Colonial Period of the Philippines. The protagonist was shown how the Spaniards abused our country and fellow countrymen.
